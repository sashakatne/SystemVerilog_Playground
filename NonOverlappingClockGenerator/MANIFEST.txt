Purpose:
  Verify the structural non-overlapping clock generator: two NAND-fed
  delay chains produce CK1 and CK2 that are guaranteed never to be high
  simultaneously, plus their complements CK1_b and CK2_b. The TB drives
  a 10 ns CK source, then over 1000 ns monitors three invariants:
    1. CK1 and CK2 never overlap (CK1 & CK2 never true)
    2. CK1 toggles
    3. CK2 toggles
    4. CK1/CK1_b are complementary; CK2/CK2_b are complementary

Source changes in this commit:
  novckgen_structural.sv:
    Added explicit `wire` declarations for delay1_out_a..e and
    delay2_out_a..e. Previously these intermediate signals were used
    without declaration, which Questa accepts via implicit wire inference
    but warns about (10x vlog-2623 'Undefined variable'). Declaring them
    silences the warnings without changing structural behaviour.

  novckgen_tb.sv:
    Verdict block: '*** FAILED ***' / '*** PASSED ***' -> 'Failed
    testbench' / 'No errors -- passed testbench', and replaced $stop with
    $finish so the script exits cleanly rather than dropping into the Tcl
    prompt at end-of-run.
