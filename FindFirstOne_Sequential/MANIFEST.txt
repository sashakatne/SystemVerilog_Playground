Purpose:
  Verify three sequential find-first-one variants of a 32-bit input bus:
    moore    FSM with all output assignments inside states (FFO32s.sv)
    mealy    Mealy variant that asserts ready combinationally on the last
             scanned bit (FFO32sMealy.sv)
    pipeline 5-cycle registered LZD-tree scanner (FFO32p.sv)
  Each variant is compared per-cycle against a KGD/predictor; the TB drives
  a 32-vector single-bit walk (Moore/Mealy) or a 37-vector queue-delayed
  walk (Pipeline) and reports a single aggregate verdict.

    > transcript_mealy.txt

    > transcript_pipeline.txt
