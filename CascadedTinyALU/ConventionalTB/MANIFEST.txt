  Verify the four-pipeline cascaded ALU (mul/add/sub/addincr/or/and/xor/not)
  end-to-end through a sequential, $finish-terminating testbench that
  exercises four directed corner blocks (21/42, 0/0, all-ones, all-minus-32768)
  plus 80,000 random ops per opcode and prints a single aggregate verdict.
